## Why CC BY-SA 4.0

This license matches the licensing approach of CASEL, the OECD Learning Compass, and most modern openly-released education standards. It permits broad reuse — including by commercial ed-tech vendors — while ensuring derivative work remains open. ShareAlike is a deliberate choice to prevent fragmentation: a vendor cannot fork OMBS into a closed proprietary version.
